Lets you select a background from the global_background_img grid, for use in the header of a page. The background will be stretched out over the full width of the browser window.

Ideal for quickly adding some design to your project. You can change the inheritance settings too by the way, or override a header background for an individual page. This ensures you always end up with the header you want.
